Need to Know

Essential info for the trip.

What do I need to travel?

A valid US passport (must not expire before December 2026). US citizens do not need a visa for Greece for stays under 90 days. You may want to register with STEP (Smart Traveler Enrollment Program).

What should I pack?

  • Swimsuits (multiple)
  • Sunscreen & sunglasses
  • Comfortable walking shoes & sandals
  • Light layers for evenings
  • Going-out outfits for dinners
  • Portable charger
  • Euros (some places are cash-only)
  • Travel adapter (Type C/F for Greece)

Villa Rules (Paros)

The Marena Estate has standard villa rules that everyone will sign off on. Key highlights:

  • No parties or gatherings beyond our group
  • Pool is unguarded — swim at your own risk
  • No unregistered guests
  • Respect quiet hours and neighbors
  • Security deposit of €1,400 is refunded if no damages

What about phone service?

Check with your carrier about international plans. Most carriers offer daily international passes ($10/day for T-Mobile, AT&T, etc.). Alternatively, pick up a local SIM or eSIM when you arrive. WiFi is available at all accommodations.

Questions?

Reach out to Ella Sklar (trip organizer) directly if you have any questions about payments, logistics, or anything else.
